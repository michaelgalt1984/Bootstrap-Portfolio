About Me

I'm Michael Nedwick. I grew up in Santa Clarita, CA (where Six Flags Magic Mountain is). I enlisted into the Marine Corps immediately after high school and spent the subsequent 9 years in the military.

I left the military in May 2012, and began attending UT Austin. After the fall semester, I decided to go to South America to travel and learn Spanish for the next 6 months. Since that point, I've spent time alternating between Texas, California and South America (principally Medellin, Colombia).

I'm now back in Austin, TX for the next 3-6 years. I'm working on forming and launching a startup, a web-based mobile platform designed to directly connect service providers (skilled and trade labor) to the consumer. I hope to launch between November 2017 to March 2018.
